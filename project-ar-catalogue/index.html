AR Catalogue

About

My work on this application was to refactor the project to make it a more scalable, flexible and easy to maintain solution. I also integrated the 3D models into the game engine and optimized the application.
I would like to highlight that in this application I developed a procedural grid that adapts to the 3D model of the product in the catalog that shows the safe area to place it.
AR Catalog is an innovative digital solution designed to increase the sales capacity of physical products. Thanks to this mobile application and the augmented reality technology it integrates, it is possible to provide customers with a life-size digital version of any product.
